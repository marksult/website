Or — you could put your ads directly in front of people who are actively searching for a personal injury attorney, in your exact location, at the exact moment they need one.

The Three Rules

The best advertising position comes down to three guidelines:

Recency — The more recently someone was injured or involved in an accident, the hotter they are as a prospect. Get ‘em while they’re hot.

Frequency — Channels where people repeatedly search for legal help signal genuine, high demand — not passive scrolling.

Unit-of-Sale — Someone searching “personal injury attorney near me” after a serious accident is a far hotter prospect than someone who clicked a generic ad. People act where their real urgency is.

And of all three, recency is by far the most important.
